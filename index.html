Creating websites has always been a very serious and painstaking job. But if you create such a website to solve significant business problems, you can't treat it somehow, "carelessly" categorically. The Internet, although it is a virtual environment, but the knowledge, effort and time spent on creating such sites are quite real. But this is not the case, since free sites are always cut down functionally, not optimized, the design is always boring and primitive, which in turn reflects on the client as someone who does not care about his reputation... The most obvious difference between working with a freelance web developer directly and working with a web studio is that the prices for services are much lower, it's like shopping in the Chinese market or in a supermarket, things are the same, but the price is different for the better for the client! As I think the only disadvantage of ordering a website from a professional freelancer is that there is no possibility of concluding a contract, since freelance web developers rarely do IP or PE, which in turn again affects the cost of creating a website and makes it less... There are a lot of freelancers providing website creation services, but there are only a few professional freelancers and there is a risk of falling into the hands of a bad specialist, which in turn will lead to a loss of time, money and nerves.
